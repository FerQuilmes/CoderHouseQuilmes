WHO WE ARE

We are the world’s biggest brewer!

Headquartered in Leuven, Belgium, we have opportunities across Europe in Belgium, the Netherlands, France, Luxembourg, Germany, Italy, the Czech Republic, the UK, Ireland, and Spain. Two key parts of our ecosystem are Budweiser Brewing Group (BBG) in the UK and Compañía Cervecera de Canarias (CCC) in the Canary Islands. We have a global ecosystem that includes:

  • 144 000 COLLEAGUES GLOBALLY
  • 137 NATIONALITIES REPRESENTED
  • >170 MAJOR BREWERIES
